ABOUT THE SOCIETY

Shree Sambhaji Raje Housing Society (HSG SOC) is a registered residential cooperative housing society located at Ganesh Nagar, Village Talwade, under the jurisdiction of Pimpri-Chinchwad Municipal Corporation (PCMC), Pune, Maharashtra. The society was established in the year 2016 with the objective of providing organized, secure, and sustainable housing facilities to its members. The society comprises 20 registered members, as per official mutation records, all of whom are recognized as lawful landlords or allottees of their respective residential units. Each member holds ownership rights in accordance with the Maharashtra Cooperative Societies Act and the society’s approved bylaws...
