Distinguished Guests

Honoring the dignitaries who graced our society

Chief Guest
26 Jan 2025

Hon. Shri. Mahesh Landge

MLA (Member of Legislative Assembly)

Visited our society on the occasion of Republic Day Flag Hoisting ceremony. He inaugurated the new Garden Gym facility and appreciated the society's cleanliness initiatives under the Swachh Bharat mission.

Special Visit
15 Aug 2024

Mrs. Smita Patil

Corporator, PCMC Ward 12

Graced the Independence Day celebration. She interacted with the residents regarding water supply improvements in Talwade and planted a sapling during the Tree Plantation Drive organized by the committee.

Religious Visit
19 Feb 2024

Shiv Jayanti Utsav

Grand Celebration

A grand procession was held to honor Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj. The event was attended by local leaders and social workers who praised the unity and cultural spirit of the Shree Sambhaji Raje Housing Society members.
