About our Society History

Shree Sambhaji Raje Housing Society (HSG SOC) is a registered residential cooperative housing society located at Ganesh Nagar, Village Talwade, under the jurisdiction of Pimpri-Chinchwad Municipal Corporation (PCMC), Pune, Maharashtra. The society was established in the year 2017 with the objective of providing organized, secure, and sustainable housing facilities to its members. The society comprises 20 registered members, as per official mutation records, all of whom are recognized as lawful landlords or allottees of their respective residential units. Each member holds ownership rights in accordance with the Maharashtra Cooperative Societies Act and the society’s approved bylaws. The society functions on democratic principles, ensuring transparency, equal participation, and collective decision-making. Shree Sambhaji Raje HSG SOC is primarily a residential housing society, designed to promote a peaceful living environment with essential civic amenities. The society actively coordinates with PCMC for municipal services such as water supply, drainage, waste management, road access, street lighting, and sanitation. Regular maintenance and upkeep of common areas are managed by the society through internally approved procedures. The society is administered by an elected Managing Committee, which is responsible for day-to-day operations, financial management, maintenance planning, and statutory compliance. Periodic general body meetings are conducted to discuss budgets, maintenance charges, repairs, and community matters. Accounts are maintained systematically, and records are updated as per cooperative norms. Located in Talwade, an area witnessing steady residential and infrastructural development, the society benefits from proximity to industrial zones, educational institutions, public transport, and essential services. The society maintains a community-focused approach, encouraging harmony, cleanliness, and mutual cooperation among residents. Overall, Shree Sambhaji Raje Housing Society stands as a well-structured and responsibly managed residential society, committed to lawful governance, member welfare, and long-term community development under the PCMC administration.

About Ganesh Nagar

Located in the serene locality of Talwade, Ganesh Nagar offers the perfect balance between industrial connectivity and residential peace. Being close to the IT Park, it is a prime location for professionals, yet it maintains a quiet, green atmosphere suitable for families and senior citizens.

Under PCMC Jurisdiction

We are proud to be under the administration of the Pimpri-Chinchwad Municipal Corporation (PCMC). Known as one of the richest and most well-managed corporations in Asia, PCMC provides us with excellent roads, regular water supply, and efficient waste management systems, ensuring a high standard of living.
